# Vehicle Tracker Auto-Sync

The tracker is deployed on Firebase Hosting and connected to Firebase Realtime Database.

## Use it

1. Open the private tracker link supplied by Codex.
2. Bookmark the complete link on your laptop.
3. Send the complete link to your phone and bookmark it there.
4. Confirm that the top button says **Synced**.

Changes made on either device update the other device automatically.

## Important

- The characters after `#sync=` are your private access key.
- Keep the complete link private.
- Do not remove the `#sync=...` portion when copying the link.
- The app retains a local copy so it can recover after a temporary connection interruption.
- Use **Export** occasionally for an additional JSON backup.
